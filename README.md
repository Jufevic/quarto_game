# Quarto game

An adaptation of the quarto board game. It's a strategy game, that is played with 16 different pieces on a 4 x 4 square grid. The goal is to align 4 pieces that share at least one common characteristic. This two player game is played turn by turn. Each turn, a player must choose wher to put the piece given by its opponent on the board, then give him a piece to play.
